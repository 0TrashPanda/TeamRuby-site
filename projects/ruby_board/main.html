RUBY BOARD


Guus - Jonah

board

a plank with a bunch of keys

Introducing the Ruby board, a custom-made keyboard designed with ergonomics and efficiency in mind. The Ruby Keyboard boasts a unique layout based on the popular Colemak design, which has been shown to improve typing speed and reduce strain on the fingers and wrists. With a split configuration, the Ruby Keyboard allows for a comfortable, natural typing position that reduces the risk of repetitive strain injuries. We understand that a keyboard is more than just a tool for typing. It's an essential component of your work environment. It's about efficiency and optimization. Whether you're a gamer, a programmer, or just someone who spends a lot of time typing, the Ruby Keyboard is the perfect tool.

Modularity

create your own setup

One of the standout features of the Ruby Keyboard is its modular design. The keyboard is designed with a master-slave configuration, which means that it can be easily expanded with additional modules like a numpad or media pad. This allows you to customize your workspace to suit your specific needs and preferences, without sacrificing comfort or functionality.

Documentation Teaser

i am too tired for this sh*t

In our quest to challenge the long-standing restrictions imposed by traditional typewriters, we embarked on the creation of a custom keyboard that breaks free from conventional design norms. Our vision materialized into a split ortholinear column-staggered ergonomic keyboard, equipped with a thumb cluster and shortcut keys. Using primarily from the Colemak layout, we developed a modified 69% layout that optimizes key placement for enhanced typing efficiency.

Our shared passion for computers and coding fueled our motivation to embark on this project. Recognizing the significant amount of time we spend on our computers and the importance of comfortable typing experiences, we set out to create a keyboard tailored to our specific needs. Moreover, the inherent intrigue and challenge of the project enticed us to dive into the realm of keyboard design and customization.
