About

we are team of talent

Welcome to Team Ruby's website! We are a group of tech enthusiasts who love creating innovative and useful gadgets that can make your daily life easier and more exciting. Our mission is to inspire you to see things from a new perspective and encourage you to explore the possibilities of technology.



Our team is passionate about designing and building unique projects that solve real-life problems. Whether it's a custom ergonomic split column staggered keyboard or a streamdeck like board, we strive to make our creations intuitive and user-friendly. With a focus on functionality and aesthetics, we aim to change the way you interact with technology.


On this website, you will find a showcase of our current projects and prototypes, as well as news and updates on the latest tech trends. We believe that sharing our ideas and experiences with the community is essential for fostering innovation and growth. We hope to inspire you to join us on our journey of exploring the endless possibilities of technology. So, sit back, relax, and explore the exciting world of Team Ruby!
